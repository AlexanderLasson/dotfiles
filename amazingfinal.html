About Pluto

Pluto is CACI’s next‑generation content management and operations platform. It integrates CMS features, telemetry ingest, and workflow management with AI‑powered assistance. Designed to run on secure internal networks, Pluto provides role‑based access, auditing, and streamlined integration with existing enterprise tools.

With HTMX, Tailwind, and Django at its core, Pluto keeps the frontend lightweight and flexible, while the backend scales with Postgres and enterprise integrations. OpenWebUI provides the local LLM interface that empowers teams to query data naturally and automate reports without leaving the CMS.

As Pluto evolves, it aims to deliver AI‑driven insights, extensible integrations, and enterprise‑grade compliance—ensuring CACI teams have a modern, secure, and intelligent platform for years to come.
