FAQ

How do you connect to Postgres?⌄
Use your existing read replica or a restricted user. We provide a minimal schema + migrations for content indexing. No write access required.
Can it run offline?⌄
Yes. HTMX keeps the UX snappy without SPA bundlers. Models can be local or proxied to approved endpoints.
What about PII and redaction?⌄
We ship rule‑based filters and allowlist prompts. Sensitive fields are masked before generation and never persisted to logs.
